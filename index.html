Course Units

Daily Routine (20 minutes)

  1. 5 min: Number talk (counting, compare more/less).
  2. 7 min: Hands-on task (patterns, sorting, shape hunt).
  3. 5 min: Competition round (below).
  4. 3 min: Reflection badge (“I kept trying!”).

Tip: celebrate effort and explanation, not only right answers.

Competition Arena

Weekly rounds reward effort, strategy, and teamwork—not just speed.

Round Types

  • Sprint Round (mental math)
  • Strategy Round (multi-step puzzles)
  • Team Relay (collaborative challenge)
  • Ontario Application Round (real-life word problems)

Scoring Model

  • Accuracy: 60%
  • Reasoning clarity: 20%
  • Persistence bonus: 10%
  • Teamwork / sportsmanship: 10%

Progression

  • House league (in-class)
  • Campus finals (monthly)
  • Regional cup (each term)
  • Ontario Junior Math Open (annual showcase)
